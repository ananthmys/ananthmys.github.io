    Vintage Mysore Tour

Saddle up, as we head out on a early morning tour when the city is waking up to life and the streets are still devoid of traffic. Get a peek into the different communities and have some chai with the locals. Take in the city at your own pace as we ride past some lovely colonial structures and get to know the stories behind them. To make up for the calories lost we end with a sumptuous South Indian breakfast and a good cuppa filter coffee on the tour

Tour Details

  • When Everyday, 7 am
  • Duration 3.5Hrs
  • Price ₹1,500/person
  • Inclusions Guide + Cycles + Helmets + Bottled Water + Chai + Breakfast
  • Meeting Place Hotel Palace Plaza, near the clock tower(Map)
